🔐 Multisig in Tonkeeper Pro

Shared wallets can turn fund transfers into a controlled, collaborative process.

When Multisig is enabled in Tonkeeper Pro, each transaction must be approved by a selected group of participants before it’s sent.

You can configure signing parameters, from requiring approval by all participants to a smaller number of approvals to confirm a transaction.

This setup works well when:
• transfers are frequent
• responsibility is shared
• transactions need to be reviewed before sending

Used by teams, DAOs, and operators managing shared or high-responsibility wallets.

💳 How to top up USDT TRC20 in Tonkeeper

USDT on TRON is supported in Tonkeeper and can be purchased directly in the app. The flow is fast and integrated with Mercuryo.

How do you purchase USDT on TRC20?

1. Open Tonkeeper and make sure USDT TRC20 is enabled in your wallet.

2. Go to Buy or Sell and find Buy USDT TRC20

3. The USDT TRC20 wallet opens with a Buy USDT button.

What appears in checkout:

- Provide your TRON address

- Mercuryo shown as the provider

- Card payment to confirm

After processing, USDT TRC20 appears in your wallet.

Note: On iOS, USDT TRC20 needs to be enabled in Settings. On Android, it’s available from the main page.
